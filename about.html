About us

My name is Sue Donym and I am the sewist behind nimble thimble.
I began sewing in 2011 as a personal hobby and soon I was making clothes for friends and relatives. In less than a year, I received multiple commissions from friends to help make outfits for them.
In 2013, the Dundrum Performance Art Academy commissioned me to make costumes for their performance of "El Cid". I've since done several other freelance projects with them and a couple other local theatres.
In 2016 my little sewing business had grown so big that I decided to expand. I now have three very talented employees, Nina, Annie and Peter whom I just couldn't run my business without.
Sewing is still very much a personal passion of mine even as it has become a full time job. We work closely with each client to figure out their needs and offer an estimate before-hand.
